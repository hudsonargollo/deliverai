## 📋 Panel Descriptions

### Customer Panels

**Menu & Ordering** (`/menu`)
- Browse all products and categories
- Add items to cart with customization options
- View product details and prices

**Order Lookup** (`/pedidos`)
- Check status of existing orders
- Enter order ID to track delivery
- View order history

**Order Flow** (`/order-flow`)
- Complete 5-step checkout process
- Step 1: Cart Review
- Step 2: Customer Info
- Step 3: Delivery Address
- Step 4: Payment Method Selection
- Step 5: Order Confirmation

## 🔐 Access Requirements

### Public Access (No Login Required)
- Home page (`/`)
- Menu (`/menu`)
- Order Lookup (`/pedidos`)
- Order Flow (`/order-flow`)
- Auth page (`/auth`)

- All `/admin/*` routes
- `/reports`

- `/print-server-config`
